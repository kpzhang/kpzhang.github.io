Biography

I am currently a Research Collaborator at Meta, an Associate Professor (with tenure) of Information Systems in the Robert H. Smith School of Business,
affiliated with AIM, MTI, and AMSC at the University of Maryland, College park.
I am also an Emerging Fellow in AI and Business Research.

Before I joined the University of Maryland, I was an Assistant professor at University of Illinois, Chicago for two years
after receiving my PhD in Computer Science at Northwestern University. During my PhD study, I co-founded 4C Insights (acquired by Mediaocean.com in 2020) with my advisor.

My research focuses on developing machine/deep learning algorithms to analyze unstructured data for better business decisions.
Specifically, I am interested in multi-modal representation learning + LLMs in business (FinTech) + Multi-agents.
