Kunpeng 'KZ' Zhang

I am an applied AI researcher. My current research interests lie in the development of artificial intelligence and representation learning in the context of business, specifically LLM in Finance and social media analytics. I am currently an Associate Professor of Information Systems at the University of Maryland, College Park and a (part time) research scientist at Meta.
